That's why people with the "skill to put things into words" are called "writers" or "essayists" and can make a living out of it.  
From elementary school kids to seasoned adults, writing is a daunting task.  
And if you want it to be grammatically correct, logically sound, error-free, and interesting too? I'm sure 80% of the population would confidently say, "I can't do that."  
Count me among that 80%, even after pretending to be a pro and making it my job for so long.  
I write on "Hobo Nikkan Itoi Shinbun" every day, and while I'm casually writing here today too, I'm sure any writing expert would be appalled at my slapdash approach.  
After all these years of writing, is this all I've got? Am I an idiot?  
Surely, writing should have been that difficult.
